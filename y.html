You stand up and attempt to orientate yourself throughout all the smoke and flames. You hear screams and multiple erratic voices all over the place. “Everyone needs to evacuate the plane!” A male voice screams from far away. Your flight instinct kicks in, and instantly you start following the voice, until you reach a group of people gathered at a safe distance far away from the plane. “Are you the last one?” You recognize the voice you have been hearing since you regained consciousness. It belongs to a well-dressed older man, but his suit is ruined with scratches and cuts. “I don’t know” You manage to say, clearly still in shock. “You might be, but we need to make sure. I will go and check the aircraft!” The man urgently responds. “You are right, I’ll go with you” you say, feeling your body overflowing from adrenaline. The man suddenly panics and says “The plane might explode at any time, it is not safe. This is not the time for you to be a hero.”
